Oven Fried Chicken

This is a classic and everybody loves some fried chicken. Its meat and crispy skin, so its a 2 in 1.

Ingredients

  • Garlic
  • Chicken
  • Sour cream
  • Cornflakes
  • Rosemary
  • Sage
  • Garlic powder
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C.
  2. Mix the items.
  3. Bake for 1 hour and be surprised.
